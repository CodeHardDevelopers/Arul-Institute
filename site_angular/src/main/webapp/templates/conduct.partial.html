The Fashion Community is an inspiring network where you can connect and share pictures of looks made of CHANEL pieces.

This community is yours! Your content is visible by the whole community. It will not be filtered by a moderator before publication, so please make sure it respects our philosophy and our guidelines. If a content published does not respect these guidelines, this will be removed.

Content of the application

DOs

  • Do publish only pictures of looks made of CHANEL pieces.
  • Do publish as many inspiring pictures of CHANEL looks as you want no matter the collections.
  • Do comment on the pictures! Be positive and constructive.
  • Do write your comments in English as much as possible.

DON’Ts

  • Do not publish pictures allowing to recognize clients and do not write any client information on the app.
  • Do not write negative comments. Everyone should feel encouraged to post & share with others.
  • Do not use or mention other fashion brands pieces and trademarks.

Use of the application

DOs

  • We invite you to use the app when you are in CHANEL boutiques or offices but you are free to use it outside.
  • In boutique, use the app in back office only when you are not looking after a client. You can use the app on the selling floor only when there is no client in the boutique (morning briefing for example)
  • Do not use or mention other fashion brands pieces and trademarks.
  • You can use the app during the buyings in the showroom. TBC
  • Feel free to share the content with CHANEL employees who are not members

DON’Ts

  • Do not show the app and its content to any client or any person who is not a CHANEL employee.
  • Do not send any app pictures to clients.
  • Do not share any pictures outside the app, such as social media platforms for example.
  • Do not use the app when you are on the selling floor while the boutique is opened
  • Do not use the app in front of a client

What you will find in this community:

Talents, Inspiration, Passion, Sharing, Respect

What you will not find in this community:

Criticisms, judgments
