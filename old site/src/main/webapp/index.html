Education is not the learning of facts, but the training of the mind to think

Confidence & hard work is the best medicine to kill the disease called failure

Arul Institute, a pioneer in supplementary education, is renowned for having trained most of the school toppers in south chennai over the last 20 years. They have an intensive, result oriented training programme for 10th training programmes are becoming indispensable to all those students aspiring to study engineering or medicine. As most students aspire for a centum in the core subjects, a thorough understanding of the concepts and extensive training in writing skills has become mandatory. The institute strives to fulfill the aspirations of the students by providing them a thorough understanding of the core subjects and the much needed practice tests. At Arul Institute, learning is made easy and enjoyable by adopting audio-visual methodology with suitable video clippings for teaching the core subjects. A projector is used for the classes where the book is saved in pdf format and all the important questions that had appeared in the public exam till date are explained in detail. This helps even the below average students to focus on the important questions for a better output. With a view to make available the best coaching even to the economically weaker section of the society, Arul Institute offers a wide range of scholarship for meritorious students. The scholarship is based on their performance in the weekly tests. Every year over 200 students were given scholarship assistance. Apart from this, full refund of tuition fees is given to those who appear regularly for weekly tests and maintain a minimum average of 90 percent.
